Admissions

Welcome to the Young International School admissions page. We are delighted that you are considering our school for your child's education. Below you will find all the information you need about our admission process, requirements, important dates, and frequently asked questions.

Application Process

  1. Step 1: Complete the online application form. Ensure all fields are filled accurately.
  2. Step 2: Submit the required documents, including a copy of the student's birth certificate and recent academic records.
  3. Step 3: Schedule an interview and assessment. This may include an entrance exam and a meeting with our admissions team.
  4. Step 4: Receive the admission decision via email. Accepted students will receive a letter of offer.
  5. Step 5: Complete enrollment and pay the registration fee to secure your child's place.

Admission Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • Copy of birth certificate
  • Most recent report card
  • Teacher recommendation letters
  • Immunization records
  • Passport-sized photographs
  • Proof of address

Important Dates

  • Application Deadline: April 30, 2024
  • Interview and Assessment Period: May 1 - May 15, 2024
  • Admission Decision Notification: June 1, 2024
  • Enrollment Deadline: June 15, 2024
  • First Day of School: August 1, 2024

FAQs

What is the age requirement for admission?

We accept students starting from age 3 for our early childhood program.

Is there an entrance exam?

Yes, an entrance exam is required for students applying to grades 1 and above. It assesses basic skills in math, English, and critical thinking.

Do you offer scholarships?

Yes, we offer scholarships based on merit and financial need. Please contact our admissions office for more information.

Can I visit the school before applying?

Absolutely! We encourage all prospective parents and students to schedule a campus tour. Contact our admissions office to arrange a visit.

What are the payment options for the registration fee?

We accept payment via bank transfer, credit card, or cash. Details will be provided upon acceptance.
